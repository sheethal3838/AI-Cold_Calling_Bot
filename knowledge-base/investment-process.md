Risk Disclosure & Important Information
Key Risks to Understand
Liquidity Risk ⚠️
Unlisted shares cannot be sold on stock exchanges
Limited buyers in secondary market
May take months to find buyer at fair price
Must hold until IPO/acquisition for optimal liquidity
Valuation Risk ⚠️
Private company valuations are subjective
Not marked-to-market daily like listed stocks
Valuation can change with funding rounds
May go up or down between purchase and exit
Information Risk ⚠️
Private companies disclose less info than public ones
Financial statements may not be audited
Management updates may be infrequent
Harder to track company performance
Company-Specific Risk ⚠️
Business may not perform as expected
Management changes can impact trajectory
Competition or regulatory challenges
Funding winter can affect valuations
No Guarantee of Returns ⚠️
Past performance ≠ future returns
Some companies may never IPO
Investment can lose value
No SEBI/government guarantee

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How long does the entire process take? A: KYC (24-48 hrs) + Order placement (instant) + Payment (instant) + Share transfer (7-10 days) = Total 10-15 days from signup to shares in demat.
Q: Can I cancel my order after payment? A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours if share transfer hasn't started. Refund processed in 3-5 business days.
Q: What if I don't have a demat account? A: We'll help you open one with our partner depositories. Takes 24 hours, completely online, free of charge.
Q: Are these investments safe? A: Your shares are legally yours in your demat account. However, unlisted investments carry market risk. Read our risk disclosure carefully.
Q: When can I sell my shares? A: You can list on our secondary marketplace anytime, but optimal exit is via IPO (18-36 months typically).
Q: Do I get the same rights as founders? A: You get standard minority shareholder rights including dividend rights, voting rights (usually limited), and participation in exits.
